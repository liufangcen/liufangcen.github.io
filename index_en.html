Biography

I received my Ph.D. degree from Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications (CQUPT), Chongqing, China in 2025, under the supervision of Chenqiang Gao(高陈强), a full professor at the School of Intelligent Systems Engineering at Sun Yat-sen University (Shenzhen Campus). Before that, I got my B.E and M.E degrees from Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2018 and 2021, respectively. I am now an Associate Professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology at the Hainan University.
I have a board range of research interests in Infrared Image Processing, Image Segmentation, Object Detection, and Multimodal Image Understanding.
